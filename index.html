About Blorp Tech

Blorp Tech is a U.S.-based software company building simplicity-first tools for field environments — where time is limited, conditions are imperfect, and clarity matters.

Our work is guided by the B.L.O.R.P. framework — Bridging Local Observations & Response Platform. Whether it’s a construction site or a disaster response environment, the people closest to the problem often have the most valuable information — yet that information rarely reaches everyone who needs it in a usable form.

We build systems that bridge that gap, translating on-the-ground observations into clear, actionable operational awareness, without burdening users with complexity or requiring ideal conditions.

In Development

LIT (Logistics Interior Tool)

LIT provides rapid interior mapping and space availability reporting for repurposed facilities (surge medical, shelters, temporary operations) when reliable floor plans don’t exist.

Sketch a simple interior layout in minutes, mark spaces as open / constrained / unavailable, and see real-time availability at a glance.

LIT is not a CAD tool. It’s built for fast operational awareness under pressure.

Our Approach

Radical Simplicity

Simplicity is not an aesthetic choice — it is an operational requirement. In the field, every unnecessary button, screen, or setting creates friction. We intentionally limit scope to what matters most so tools remain usable under stress.

Real-World Reliability

We design for degraded conditions by default. Our systems are built to function in low-bandwidth, low-power, and high-stress environments. Features that cannot be relied upon in real-world conditions are intentionally excluded.

Human-Centered Design

We design for people, not systems — crews working in motion, civilians under stress, responders making rapid decisions, and volunteers coordinating with limited information. Our goal is technology that supports human judgment rather than competing with it.

Operational Clarity

When everything goes wrong at once, clarity becomes a lifeline. Our products transform fragmented information into simple, real-time understanding — the right data, in the right form, at the right moment.
