Privacy Policy

Last updated:

This Privacy Policy describes how Blorp Tech LLC ("Blorp Tech," "we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our website and products, including Jobsite Live, Disaster Help, and LIT (Logistics Interior Tool).

1. Information We Collect

a. Account Information

When using our applications, you may provide basic information such as name, email address, company affiliation, or role within an organization.

b. Location Data

Certain products (such as Jobsite Live and Disaster Help) may collect location data to enable operational features such as geofence awareness, hazard reporting, or location-based coordination. Location access is requested through your device and may be disabled at any time in your device settings.

c. User-Submitted Content

Our products may allow users to submit information such as hazard reports, help requests, operational updates, or interior space mappings. This information is stored to support coordination and operational awareness.

d. Device & Technical Information

We may collect limited technical information such as device type, operating system, and application version to improve reliability and performance.

e. Website Information

When visiting blorptech.com, basic non-personal analytics information (such as browser type or page visits) may be collected to improve site functionality.

2. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain our products
  • Enable real-time coordination features
  • Improve reliability and performance
  • Respond to inquiries or demo requests
  • Ensure security and prevent misuse

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information.

Information may be shared within an organization’s authorized users as part of normal product functionality (e.g., jobsite teams or emergency responders).

We may share information if required by law or to protect the safety, rights, or property of users or the public.

4. Data Retention

Data is retained only as long as necessary to support operational functionality, comply with legal obligations, or maintain system integrity. Certain operational reports may expire automatically depending on product configuration.

5. Security

We implement reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no system can be guaranteed 100% secure.

6. Your Choices

You may:

  • Disable location access through your device settings
  • Request deletion of your account (if applicable)
  • Contact us with privacy-related questions

7. Children's Privacy

Our products are not directed toward children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected by revising the "Last updated" date above.
